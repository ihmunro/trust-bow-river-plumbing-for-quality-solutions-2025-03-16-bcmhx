About Bow River Plumbing

Founded in 2010, Bow River Plumbing began with a simple mission: to provide Calgary residents with reliable, professional plumbing services they could trust. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most respected plumbing companies, serving thousands of satisfied customers across residential and commercial sectors.

Our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in transparent pricing, professional expertise, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Our team of certified plumbers undergoes regular training to stay current with the latest technologies and techniques, ensuring we deliver the highest quality service possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We provide comprehensive plumbing services throughout Calgary and surrounding areas, including Airdrie, Cochrane, and Okotoks. Our service area covers a 50km radius from downtown Calgary.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We offer 24/7 emergency service with an average response time of 60 minutes or less. Our emergency team is always ready to handle urgent plumbing issues, including burst pipes, severe leaks, and sewer backups.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive warranty on all our services. Labor is guaranteed for 1 year, and we honor manufacturer warranties on all parts and fixtures we install. Additionally, we offer extended warranty options for major installations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, e-transfers, and cash. For larger projects, we also offer flexible financing options with approved credit. Commercial clients can apply for net-30 payment terms.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for all standard plumbing services. For complex installations or commercial projects, we offer detailed written quotes after a thorough on-site assessment. All quotes are valid for 30 days.
