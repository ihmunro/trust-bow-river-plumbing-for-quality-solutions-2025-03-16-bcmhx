Essential Home Plumbing Maintenance Tips

Protect your home's plumbing system and avoid costly repairs with essential maintenance practices. Regular inspections help detect leaks and damage early, while proper drain care prevents clogs and extends system longevity. Monitor water pressure to prevent strain on pipes, and maintain your water heater through regular flushing and component checks. Winter-proof your plumbing to prevent frozen pipes, and implement water conservation measures to reduce system stress. While many tasks can be handled independently, annual professional inspections are crucial for identifying potential issues. Follow a structured maintenance schedule to keep your plumbing running efficiently year-round.
